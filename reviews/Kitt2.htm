Giovanni Fantini

What did you like the most?

- The team <3
Especially the following (in random order):
1) Arthur always knowing the answer
2) Ben being the 4th addition to my team
3) Phelim's patience and determination
4) Jess and Sandrine's frontend magic
5) Ed's ethernal positivity
- The network: it has so much potential and provides access to so many talented people. I give more details on how I think these could be leveraged more in subsequent sections
- The parties and generally the chilled environment. I have found so many new friends!

What did you like the least?

- Having the lectures everyday at the same time. It would have been great if on some days lecture would start at different times to allow people with other entrepreneurial goals to follow on some occasions (i.e. monday, wednesday and friday start at 9am, tuesday and thursday start at 12pm)
- The little "theory" included in the course's syllabus. I might be one of the few but I would have loved to have more "web 101" type of lectures explaining more the theory behind some topics such as security, browser support, code efficiency, benchmarking etc.
- The little incentives to put effort in the exercises. I would guess some kind of gamification concept during the batch with a points system that discloses the best coders each week (obviously not the bottom) to incentivise the class to always do better
- The little control people have over their buddy and team assignments. I think you should give the chance to students to actually say when they didn’t find themselves well working with some particular buddies to avoid forcing people to work with people you don’t get along with or with whom you don’t see value in working together (doesn’t necessarily mean with people who are “slower” coders, just people who don’t necessarily have a work approach you agree with). This is especially important in the formation of the teams (especially for people who are actually building a startup / real project) to avoid people getting assigned people they are not getting along well. I think once people vote after the pitches, you should give captains some say in who they would prefer to get before running the selecting algorithm. My team was great and I was so lucky, but I hear other people who weren’t necessarily as happy

I am very keen to help in building these and any other improvements Le Wagon might deem valuable in my spare time
