What did you like the most?

The atmosphere of support and motivation was amazing. I haven't been in a learning environment in such a long time and to feel at ease and to be bolstered every time I felt I wasn't up to it was great.

What did you like the least?

I could have done with a few more reboots and a few less live codes. To be totally honest I never really learnt that much in the live codes, it made me feel on edge and I couldn't really concentrate on what was going on. I would have found reboots better on the lots of the days where the exercises were a bit more challenging.

Your feedback about Sandrine

Sandrine was amazing. She was the first female lecturer we had and her skills were very inspiring! One on one she was always really patient and made sure I understood how she was fixing something. Nothing bad to say!

Your feedback about Arthur

Really enjoyed his lectures and found the initial reboot massively helpful. One on one he found and fixed issues really quickly, sometimes a tiny bit too quickly for me to keep up but thats more my problem. Arthur overall is just really great, if I'm ever even a 1/4 as good as him at coding I'll be happy!

Your feedback about Benjamin

Ben encouraged me to think for myself. Aside from the lectures he did which were really clear I had this one really long ticket with him for setting up PayPal on our app and I honestly have never felt more like an idiot but I came out feeling like I had actually worked through the problem with him and that I had understood what was wrong. He actually asked me questions rather than letting me sit in silence whilst he fixed the issue which I think some of the other teachers could do more!

Your feedback about Phelim

Phelim is the best. He has the nicest style of lecturing, very relaxed and super clear and I took a lot away from them. I wish we could have had him more!

Your feedback about Romain

Romain helped so much with our app, he sat on some really long tickets with me and other people on the team. It felt like he actually really cared about it and enjoyed working through the problems which was really encouraging. My only negative is that in the lectures I sometimes felt like he could go off on tangents, they were really interesting tangents but as the lectures can feel quite long anyway sometimes I think it made it hard to focus!

Your feedback about Sam

Sam literally puts us all to shame! Such a nice guy, really patient with tickets and amazing at coding.

Your feedback about Guy

I reallly liked Guy, he was really enthusiastic and so nice. Clearly a bit of a genius but he never made me feel like an idiot. Nothing bad to say!

Your feedback about Leonard

Leonard was really kind and so encouraging, I didn't have many tickets with him but just generally throughout the course he would check in with how I was feeling and make sure I didn't quit which was much needed!

Your feedback about Ben

Whenever I had tickets with Ben I came away completely understanding the problem and solution. Honestly the best TA!

Your feedback about Hedi

Hedi was so kind and I really enjoyed having tickets with her because she would often completely understand my thought processes when trying to tackle a problem. She would talk through the solution to problem and instead of just giving me the answer she would leave me to make the changes which really allowed me to learn!

Your feedback about Le Wagon's London staff?

I've got nothing negative to say about the staff at Le Wagon, they were all welcoming, kind and fun. Considering they do the same 9 weeks over and over again, they really made you feel like they were as enthusiastic about you as anyone else before!

As an alumni, what do you expect now from Le Wagon?

I would love to maintain the sense of community we have as a batch and to get some support in the job hunt.

How would you sum up your experience at Le Wagon?

It was a massive risk to leave my business behind and totally change my career with this course but I am so glad I did. I can't believe the amount I've learnt, I am so surprised that anything actually clicked into place like everyone kept saying it would and its left me feeling excited about the future and really sad that it's over.

Sorry for this overly positive and not very constructive feedback but thank you for the best experience ever!
